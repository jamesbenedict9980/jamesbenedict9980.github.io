DESIGNER TIPS  

1One of the most popular trends for floors and walls involves a play on pattern. Incorporate herringbone installs, contrasting grout colors or texture for ultimate tile style.


2For something unexpected, mix two or three colors of wood look planks together to create a dramatic look that will make your modern makeover one-of-a-kind.


3Use tile in unconventional ways, two story tile fire places, 3D tile accent walls, and tile moving outdoors will be all the rage this year.

DELLANO

Our Dellano rectified tile collection features a contemporary wood-look crafted from fine polished porcelain. Imported from Spain, this wood look tile collection is anything but ordinary. Featuring a trio of trend-worthy colors—rich browns, exotics blues, and gorgeous grays—its distinguished polished finish turns-up the wow factor in residential and commercial spaces. Offered in fashionable 8x48 planks, Dellano is fabulous as flooring, but it’s also an eye-catcher on walls, countertops, and backsplashes. Able to go where real wood can’t, use this next generation wood-look plank in the kitchen, bath, mud room, or laundry area—the choice is yours and the possibilities are endless.

HAVENWOOD

The Havenwood Collection is offered in four distinctly subtle colorways, each replicating the look of freshly cut timber. Available in 8x36 planks and a chic 12x15 chevron mosaic, Havenwood is as versatile as it is gorgeous. Use it in residential or commercial environments on floors, accent walls, backsplashes, or anywhere you want to achieve a contemporary wood look. Pleasingly neutral, Havenwood coordinates effortlessly with any number of other materials, for one-of-a-kind installations that are sure to garner attention.

BELMOND

Wood looks that wow! Our Belmond Collection marries the trendiest neutral colors with contemporary style for a fashion-forward combo that complements almost any décor. DOMINO

Domino Porcelain decorative mosaics put your love of all things retro on display in your kitchen and bath. A modern take on the classic checkerboard, Domino is offered in white, black, and gray hues with polished and matte finishes. Give your living spaces and backsplashes a vintage refresh with a variety of styles – including subway, hexagons, arabesques, and penny rounds. Achieve a polished look with Domino’s coordinating floor tiles.
