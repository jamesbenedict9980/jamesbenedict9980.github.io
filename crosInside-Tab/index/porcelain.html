BERNINI

A luxurious linear look meets lovely neutrals and one-of-a-kind details in Bernini. Bernini Porcelain makes a style statement with its unique vein cut travertine look and fossilized veining in the most desired hues of whites, creams, and warm grays. Available in polished and matte finishes, this glazed tile collection offers several sizes – 12x24, 18x18, even 4x18 – along with a coordinating decorative mosaic and bullnose to bring a touch of artistic style to your space.

DOMINO

Domino Porcelain puts your love of all things retro on display in your kitchen and bath. Our vintage-inspired checkerboard collection pairs black and white tiles together for a classic yet modern look. Or select all-white or all-black tiles for a chic monochromatic design. Perfect for residential and commercial spaces, Domino offers several formats – including popular 12x24 and large format 24x24 and 32x32 sizes – along with coordinating decorative mosaics to complete the retro look.

TEKTILE

Tektile pairs an Italian glazed porcelain tile with all the texture you’d expect from a modern fabric-inspired collection. This ultra-durable and low-maintenance new concept line features three styles—a nod to the fabric, linen, and denim looks we love—in ivory and gray tones. An intriguing departure from other floor and wall tiles, you can mix and match them to give a wide range of living and commercial spaces a trendy look.

COUNTRY RIVER

Country River’s burnt wood look and rich textured finish harken back to traditional Colonial wood floors. Available in four gorgeous brown and grey colors, these large format porcelain wood planks will bring classic style to your space.

VINTAGE

Vintage Porcelain combines the aesthetic qualities of aged barn wood with the practical benefits of porcelain to give a wide range of living spaces instant appeal. Designed for both residential and commercial installations, our remarkably durable and family-friendly Vintage line features a scraped painted finish affect reminiscent of reclaimed timber. Now offered in four on-trend color schemes including white and black, the unique wide planks have soft pillowed edges and large format 8x36 size.

SIERRA

Nature meets designer style in Sierra. Sierra Porcelain delivers a unique, artistic wood look tile that complements contemporary spaces. Available in three beautiful colors, each large format 9x48 plank fuses different wood look pieces together for incredible variation and statement style.

CAROLINA TIMBER

Bring a bit of exotic style to your space with Carolina Timber. Featuring four rich colors in 6x24 planks, this gorgeous ceramic wood look tile line offers the look and feel of Brazilian tigerwood with a hand-rubbed oil finish.

ASPENWOOD

At the lake house. In an urban loft. Wall-to-wall in a small-town bungalow. Aspenwood Porcelain looks at home everywhere. Designed with the rich tones and textures of exotic wood, the tile collection features modern color glazes, intricate knots, and authentic-looking grains. A super durable maintenance-free line, our large format 9x48 porcelain planks instantly modernizes your floors, walls, and backsplashes.
