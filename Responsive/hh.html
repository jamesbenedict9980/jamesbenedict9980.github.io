Welcome to Talent Zone

Talent Zone HR Services is leading recruitment process outsourcing and consulting organization. The company is managed by an experienced team of professionals who have served leading organizations. Established in 2014, Talent Zone HR Services has helped more clients in finding the right talent and helped more than 500 candidates to find their dream jobs since inception. Working across Industry Verticals and Functional Areas, we are trusted to deliver fast, efficient and caring service to clients and candidates alike. Talent Zone HR Services has won accolades and business from existing and new clients for our consultative recruitment approach. We have been helping our clients to improve their recruitment process in terms of Quality-of-Hire, Time-to-Fill and Cost-per-Hire. We specialize in providing customized hiring solutions to our clients so that they can achieve greater efficiency and business performance. Through our collaborative approach and flawless execution we help our clients achieve their strategic talent acquisition goals. Our passion, integrity and work ethics set us apart and make us a preferred hiring partner with our ever expanding client base. We seek to deliver excellent value to our clients and candidates alike with our highly professional services.

Vision

Mission

About us:

Talent Zone HR Services, is a licensed HR Services company. We specialize in the Recruitment and staffing and selection programs that are tailored to meet the business objectives and relevant demands of our clients marketplaces. Talent Zone HR Services Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2014 to provide end to end HR solutions to corporate firms. Talent Zone HR Services focuses on the delivery of Permanent Recruitment, Contract Staffing, Mass Recruitments, Reference Checks and Employee Validation for our clients. Talent Zone HR Services is positioned to cater to a company's contract staffing needs on all the verticals.
