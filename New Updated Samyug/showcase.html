Text & Document Processing

80% enterprise data is unstructured, In the form of Documents, Email, Text, Logs Complex nature of the data and the challenges , achieving accuracy and costs in processing has lead Enterprise to under leverage unstructured Data for the enterprise. Documents exist in the form of Legal - Contracts, Communication, Information, Research – Reports, Interactions – Chats & Machine Logs in PDF, Office, Email and log formats.


Our framework takes a collection of documents and extracts content retaining the original information, structure and context. A pipeline of frameworks then extract, objects, Data- Types, meta-data & dictionary driven Entities finally Deriving Key-Value pairs. These individual text fragments are then processed for NLP – for sentiment and Association. These then can be useful for intelligence or downstream models.

Applications for Document Extraction, Document Classification, Document comparison, Times-series Sequence Analyses, Sentiment and Association.
